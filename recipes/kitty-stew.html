Kitty Stew

A delightful joy to munch!

This dish is not what you'd expect. It's cute and cuddly. It has it's chirps and fur, but overall enjoyable!

Ingredients

  • Your local kachew
  • Szechaun sauce
  • Thicc fluffy pancakes
  • A woof wooof
  • Sprinkled mouse
  • Go Gurt

Recipe Instructions

  1. Pet your subject softly
  2. Grate the Gurt
  3. Dig out a spot in the pancakes for mousie
  4. Back and forth back and forth with the sauce
  5. Woof woof to top it off
  6. Toss the project into an iron pot
  7. Stir for 48 hours and season with neighbors
